# NPT Software House

## Overview

NPT Software House is a cutting-edge software development organization specializing in AI-based products and services. We operate with an autonomous agent-driven team structure where specialized agents collaborate to deliver high-quality software solutions.

## Mission

Build reliable, scalable AI software products with strong delivery discipline and clear operational transparency.

## Core Values

- **Deterministic Handoff**: Clear output locations, commit hashes, and test coverage
- **Short Review Loops**: Maximum 2 revision cycles per task
- **Low Organizational Noise**: Direct communication, clear responsibilities
- **Strong Execution**: Shipping over decoration

## Organization Structure

The company operates through a specialized agent team, each with distinct capabilities:

### Leadership
- **CEO**: Strategic leadership and high-level decisions
- **CTO**: Technical architecture and engineering strategy

### Product & Business
- **Product Manager**: Product vision, roadmap, and stakeholder coordination
- **Delivery/Release Manager**: Artifact quality and final handoff verification

### Engineering
- **Software Engineer**: Full-stack development (backend, frontend, AI integration)
- **ML Engineer**: Machine learning models, training, deployment
- **UI Developer**: Frontend UI, design systems, user experience

### Quality
- **QA Critic**: Quality assurance, review cycles, delivery standards enforcement

**Status**: MVP Complete, In Development

**Technology Stack**: FastAPI, React/Three.js, PyTorch (planned)

### 2. Onboarding Platform
**Status**: In Planning

## Operating Principles

1. **Autonomous Agents**: Each agent has clear capabilities and operates independently within their domain
2. **Delivery Gate**: All work must meet delivery standards before completion
3. **Test-Driven Development**: Comprehensive test coverage required
4. **Documentation First**: README, limitations, and setup instructions mandatory
5. **Fast Iteration**: Short cycles with rapid feedback loops
